Q&A

Can I boot from SD card?

Yes. Press ESC after powering on.

Tip: it helps to turn off “quiet boot” in the BIOS (F2 to get there).


Can I use multiple Android devices with a single Google account?

Yes.


Can I setup multiboot with android-x86?

Yes. The installer recognized my Win7 partition and asked if I want an entry for it in the GRUB boot menu.


Can I use an USB bluetooth dongle?

I have tried it with a Cambridge Silicon Radio dongle. Just plug it in, turn on bluetooth in the settings and I could transfer a file to my SGS.


Can I use an external VGA monitor or TV?

You need to disable the internal display by adding this to the kernel cmdline (in GRUB):


video=LVDS-1:d video=1024x768

d – means “disable”.
